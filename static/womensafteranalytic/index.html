Person Detection & Gender Classification

Our advanced surveillance system can automatically detect individuals in any given scene. By utilizing deep learning models, the system identifies the gender of each person in real-time. This feature is crucial in monitoring public spaces to ensure that women are not in potentially unsafe environments.

Whether in crowded places like subway stations or isolated areas, detecting the presence and distribution of different genders can provide vital information for law enforcement and help in maintaining a secure environment for women.

Gender Distribution

Understanding the gender distribution in a specific area is essential for evaluating the safety of that environment. This feature counts the number of men and women present in a scene, offering a clear overview of gender balance or imbalance.

A higher concentration of men in certain scenarios, such as late at night in isolated areas, can be a cause for concern. By monitoring these metrics, authorities can take proactive measures to ensure the safety of women, particularly in high-risk locations.

Identifying a Lone Woman at Night

Nighttime can pose increased risks for women, particularly when they are alone in public spaces. This feature is designed to monitor such situations by detecting the presence of a lone woman during late hours.

The system uses a combination of video analytics and contextual information, such as the time of day and location, to flag potentially vulnerable situations. Early detection of these scenarios allows for timely intervention, ensuring that help can be provided if necessary.

Detection of a Woman Surrounded by Men

This feature is designed to detect scenarios where a woman is surrounded by a group of men, which could be a sign of a potentially unsafe situation. The system continuously analyzes video feeds to identify such patterns and raises an alert if the situation is deemed suspicious.

In crowded environments, such as public transport or events, this feature ensures that any unusual gathering around a woman is quickly detected, allowing for a rapid response from security personnel.

Recognizing SOS Situation

Our SOS detection system is equipped with gesture recognition technology to identify distress signals. Whether it's a specific hand gesture or another predefined action, the system is trained to recognize these indicators and trigger an immediate alert to authorities.

This feature is particularly useful in situations where verbal communication is not possible, such as during an abduction or a threatening encounter. By recognizing these subtle signals, the system ensures that help is on the way as quickly as possible.
